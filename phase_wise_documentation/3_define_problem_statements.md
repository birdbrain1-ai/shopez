# Ideation Phase
## Define the Problem Statements
* **Date**: 18 June 2026
* **Project Name**: Shopez

### Customer Problem Statement Template
A well-articulated customer problem statement allows the team to find the ideal solution for the challenges customers face.

#### Problem Statement 1 (PS-1): The Customer Perspective
* **I am**: An online retail shopper named Aashritha.
* **I’m trying to**: Securely purchase items from the web store using my credit card.
* **But**: Traditional checkout forms are long, boring, and do not provide clear visual feedback about input errors.
* **Because**: They lack interactive visual representations of cards and real-time validations.
* **Which makes me feel**: Reluctant to finish the purchase, leading to high cart abandonment rates.

#### Problem Statement 2 (PS-2): The Administrator Perspective
* **I am**: A store inventory manager.
* **I’m trying to**: Track sales performance, view recent orders, and update stock counts easily.
* **But**: Standard backend databases often fail, causing the entire platform to crash, and admin dashboards often display text in poor contrast modes.
* **Because**: There is no automatic database fallback mechanism and styling issues persist in dark/white text elements.
* **Which makes me feel**: Frustrated and unable to process client orders or analyze store performance.
